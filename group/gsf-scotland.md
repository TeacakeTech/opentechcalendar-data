The Green Software – Scotland group is for anyone interested in green software. We look forward to seeing you at our next event! Our events are held in various locations across Scotland, usually Edinburgh or Glasgow. 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁳󠁣󠁴󠁿💚

The Green Software Foundation is a non-profit with the mission to create a trusted ecosystem of people, standards, tooling and best practices for green software. The Green Software Foundation aims to change the culture of building software across the tech industry, so sustainability becomes a core priority to software teams, just as important as performance, security, cost and accessibility. The Foundation's mission is to reduce the total change in global carbon emissions associated with software. Ensuring that all of our members and contributors feel that they can actively contribute will ensure that we create an environment that promotes belonging and will lead to better outcomes.

More info available at:
https://greensoftware.foundation/
