IOT North - Scotland Meetup
AIoTHub (formerly IoT North) Community meetup are pleased to present Suyash Joshi in "Physical AI at the Edge: Real Time Intelligence with Sensors and Data"

Abstract:
Physical AI brings intelligence into the real world through sensors and devices that observe, decide, and act in real time. Making this work requires processing data where it’s generated at the edge. In this talk, we’ll explore how **Physical AI systems use real-time sensor data and edge computing** to deliver low latency intelligence. The session includes a **live edge AI Robotic demo**, with **open source code and hardware instructions on GitHub**. You’ll leave with practical patterns for building realtime Physical AI systems at the edge.

Bio:
Suyash Joshi is a Senior Developer Advocate at InfluxData, focused on AI and data engineering. He brings 15 years of experience across Oracle, RingCentral and InfluxData. He regularly speaks at technical conferences on building practical, real-time systems that connect data to the physical world. In his free time, he leads the Magineer London meetup (3,400+ members) and serves as a UK STEM Ambassador.
